Summary of Candidate Clavata Homologs for the Four-Leaf Clover Trait

Key Findings:

  • CLAVATA1 (CLV1) and BARELY ANY MERISTEM 3 (BAM3): Important receptor-like kinases involved in meristem function and organ number regulation.
  • Homologs: NODULE AUTOREGULATION RECEPTOR KINASE (NARK), HYPERNODULATION ABERRANT ROOT FORMATION 1 (HAR1), and SUPER NUMERIC NODULES (SUNN).
  • Relevance: These genes may influence leaf morphology and the four-leaf trait in white clover.
